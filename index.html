Common Questions

When sharing personal documents online for verification purposes, watermarks protect against identity theft and document misuse. They ensure your documents can only be used for their intended purpose while remaining readable for legitimate verification.

Yes. SafeID runs entirely in your web browser. Your documents are processed locally on your device using JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas. No files are uploaded to any server, and no data leaves your computer.

SafeID works with any image-based document: government-issued IDs, passports, driver's licenses, academic diplomas, professional certificates, legal contracts, medical records, and financial documents. Supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats.
